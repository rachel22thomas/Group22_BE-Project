About Our Project


Many drivers in the country sacrifice sleep, an often overlooked and dangerous behaviour that results in majority of them being sleep-deprived while behind the wheel every day. Sleepiness decreases our vigilance, response time, memory and decision making which are all essential ingredients of safe driving.
Compared to a breath analyzer used to detect drunk driving we have no definitive detection system for drowsiness which is a clear drawback in treating drowsiness as an offense by law. Hence, with the help of Computer Vision techniques, we have created a system that monitors the driver and notifies him to wake up when he is drowsy.
